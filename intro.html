Introduction

Principles and Techniques of Data Science by NYU CDS

By NYU CDS

This is the textbook for Data 100, the Principles and Techniques of Data Science course at UC Berkeley.

Data 100 is the upper-division, semester-long data science course that follows DS-UA 111 Data Science for Everyone. The reader's assumed background is detailed in the About This Book page.
